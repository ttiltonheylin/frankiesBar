About Frankie's

Quintessential Sports Bar Vibes.

This is Downtown Dallas' very own living room!

Frankie's has been around Dallas for 20 years! Owned and operated by a husband and wife team, a Black Hawk Pilot and a CPA. Bill and Johnnie's sense of hospitality create the foundation for the staff at Frankie's to provide outstanding experiences.
